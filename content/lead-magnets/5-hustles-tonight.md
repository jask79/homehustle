## Hustle #1: Micro-Tasks & Freelance Gigs
**Setup time:** 10-15 minutes  
**First payment:** 24-48 hours  
**Earning potential:** $5-50 per task

### What You'll Do
Complete small tasks: data entry, research, writing short descriptions, categorizing images, transcription, and more.

### Step-by-Step Setup

**Option A: Amazon Mechanical Turk**
1. Go to **mturk.com/worker**
2. Click "Get Started with Amazon Mechanical Turk"
3. Sign in with your Amazon account (or create one)
4. Complete worker registration
5. Wait for approval (usually instant to 48 hours)
6. Start accepting HITs (Human Intelligence Tasks)

*Pro tip: Install the "MTurk Suite" browser extension to find better-paying tasks.*

**Option B: Clickworker**
1. Go to **clickworker.com**
2. Click "Earn money as a Clickworker"
3. Create your account
4. Complete the assessment tests (they determine which jobs you see)
5. Start working immediately after passing

**Option C: Appen**
1. Go to **appen.com/join-our-crowd**
2. Create a profile
3. Apply to projects that match your skills
4. Complete qualification tests
5. Get assigned to projects (pays weekly via PayPal)

### Earnings Breakdown
- Casual (1 hour/day): $5-15/day
- Active (2-3 hours/day): $15-40/day
- Power user (4+ hours/day): $40-100/day

## Hustle #2: Sell Your Stuff
**Setup time:** 15-30 minutes  
**First payment:** 24 hours - 1 week  
**Earning potential:** $50-500+ this week

### What You'll Do
Turn clutter into cash. You have things worth money — guaranteed.

### Step-by-Step Setup

**For Local Sales (Facebook Marketplace):**
1. Open Facebook app
2. Tap the Marketplace icon (storefront)
3. Tap "Sell" then "Item for Sale"
4. Take 3-5 photos (good lighting, clean background)
5. Write title + description (be specific)
6. Set price (check similar items first)
7. Post in your area

**For Clothes (Poshmark):**
1. Download Poshmark app
2. Create account
3. Tap the camera icon to list
4. Photo, describe, price, post
5. Share your listings daily (increases visibility)

**For Electronics (Swappa):**
1. Go to **swappa.com**
2. Create a seller account
3. List your phone/tablet/laptop
4. Ship after sale (they handle payment safely)

### What Sells Fast
- Smartphones (even cracked screens have value)
- Gaming consoles & games
- Brand-name clothes/shoes
- Furniture
- Power tools
- Fitness equipment

### Pro Tips
- Price 20% below retail for fast sales
- Respond to messages within 30 minutes
- Meet buyers at police station parking lots (safe & free)

## Hustle #3: Food/Grocery Delivery
**Setup time:** Sign up tonight, approved by tomorrow  
**First payment:** Weekly direct deposit  
**Earning potential:** $15-25/hour

### What You'll Do
Pick up food or groceries, deliver to customers, collect tips.

### Step-by-Step Setup

**DoorDash:**
1. Go to **doordash.com/dasher/signup**
2. Enter your info and vehicle type
3. Consent to background check
4. Wait for approval (usually 1-3 days)
5. Activate your Red Card when it arrives (or use digital version)
6. Go online and start accepting orders

**Uber Eats:**
1. Download the Uber Driver app
2. Sign up to deliver
3. Upload required documents (license, insurance, registration)
4. Complete background check
5. Once approved, go online and deliver

**Instacart:**
1. Go to **shoppers.instacart.com**
2. Sign up as a Full-Service Shopper
3. Complete background check
4. Wait for approval
5. Download the Shopper app and start accepting batches

### Maximizing Your Earnings
- **Work peak hours:** Lunch (11am-1pm), Dinner (5pm-9pm), Weekends
- **Cherry-pick orders:** Decline low-paying, long-distance deliveries
- **Multi-app:** Run DoorDash and Uber Eats simultaneously
- **Track mileage:** Use an app like Stride or Everlance (tax deduction)

### Real Example
Friday night, 6pm-9pm (3 hours):
- 6 deliveries × $8 average = $48 base
- Tips: $20-30
- **Total: $68-78 for 3 hours = ~$24/hour**

## Hustle #4: Paid Surveys & User Studies
**Setup time:** 5-10 minutes per platform  
**First payment:** Same day - 1 week  
**Earning potential:** $1-100 per task

### What You'll Do
Share opinions, test websites/apps, participate in research.

### Step-by-Step Setup (Do All of These Tonight)

**1. UserTesting ($10-60 per test)**
1. Go to **usertesting.com/get-paid-to-test**
2. Create account
3. Take the sample test (required)
4. Install their browser extension
5. Get notified when tests match your profile

**2. Respondent ($50-300 per study)**
1. Go to **respondent.io**
2. Sign up with LinkedIn (recommended) or email
3. Complete your profile thoroughly
4. Apply to studies that match you
5. Get selected → Complete interview → Get paid

**3. Prolific ($6-15/hour)**
1. Go to **prolific.co**
2. Create account
3. Complete the "About You" section (very important!)
4. Leave the tab open — studies appear throughout the day
5. Click fast — good studies fill up in seconds

**4. dscout ($25-200 per mission)**
1. Download the dscout app
2. Create account
3. Apply to "missions" (multi-day research projects)
4. If selected, complete tasks and get paid

### Pro Tips
- **Complete your profile 100%** — More surveys match detailed profiles
- **Be consistent** — Contradictory answers get you banned
- **Check frequently** — Best-paying studies disappear fast
- **Use multiple platforms** — Never rely on just one

### Realistic Weekly Earnings
- 30 min/day: $25-50/week
- 1 hour/day: $50-100/week
- 2+ hours/day: $100-200/week
- Plus occasional high-paying studies: +$50-300

## Hustle #5: Pet Sitting & Dog Walking
**Setup time:** 15-20 minutes  
**First booking:** 1-7 days  
**Earning potential:** $15-50 per walk/visit

### What You'll Do
Walk dogs, pet-sit, do drop-in visits. Perfect if you love animals.

### Step-by-Step Setup

**Rover:**
1. Go to **rover.com/become-a-sitter**
2. Create your sitter profile
3. Add photos (you + any pets you have)
4. Write your bio (friendly, trustworthy, experienced)
5. Set your services and rates
6. Pass the background check
7. Get listed and start receiving requests

**Wag:**
1. Download the Wag! app
2. Apply to become a walker
3. Complete the application + quiz
4. Pass background check
5. Get approved and start claiming walks

### Setting Your Rates
- Dog walking (30 min): $15-25
- Drop-in visit (30 min): $15-20
- Day sitting: $25-45
- Overnight sitting: $45-75

*Start at the lower end, raise prices after you get 5+ reviews.*

### Getting Your First Bookings
- Complete your profile 100%
- Add 5+ photos (you with dogs, your home)
- Write a compelling bio
- Set competitive initial rates
- Respond to requests within 30 minutes
- Offer "meet and greet" before first booking

### Real Example
Weekend dog-sitting:
- Friday 6pm → Sunday 6pm = 2 nights
- Rate: $60/night × 2 = $120
- Plus walks: $15 × 4 = $60
- **Total: $180 for one weekend gig**
