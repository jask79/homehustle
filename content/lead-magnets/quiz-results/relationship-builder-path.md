## Your Top 4 Opportunities

### 1. Affiliate Marketing ($500-10,000+/month)
**Best for:** People who love recommending things

**How It Works:**
You recommend products. People buy through your link. You earn commission (5-50% of sale).

**Getting Started:**
1. Pick a niche you're passionate about (fitness, tech, finance, etc.)
2. Start a simple content channel (TikTok, YouTube, blog, email list)
3. Join affiliate programs (Amazon Associates, ShareASale, individual brands)
4. Create honest reviews and recommendations
5. Build trust → Make sales → Earn commissions

**High-Commission Programs:**
- Software (20-50% recurring): Notion, ConvertKit, Teachable
- Finance (up to $200 per signup): Credit cards, investing apps
- Courses ($50-500 per sale): Skillshare, Masterclass
- Physical products (3-10%): Amazon, Target, Walmart

### 2. Remote Sales ($50,000-150,000+/year)
**Best for:** Competitive people who want uncapped income

**Types of Remote Sales:**
- **SDR (Sales Development Rep):** Set appointments, $40-60K base + bonus
- **Account Executive:** Close deals, $60-100K base + commission
- **High-Ticket Closer:** Close expensive products, commission-only but lucrative

**Getting Started:**
1. Take a free sales course (HubSpot Sales Certification)
2. Apply to remote sales jobs on LinkedIn, AngelList, We Work Remotely
3. Start as SDR if no experience (learn the ropes)
4. Move to closing roles within 6-12 months
5. Top performers earn $100-200K+

**Industries Hiring:**
- SaaS (software companies)
- Real estate
- Insurance
- Financial services
- Course creators/coaches

### 3. Real Estate Referrals ($500-5,000 per referral)
**Best for:** People who know a lot of people

**How It Works:**
You refer someone buying or selling a home to an agent. If they close, you get a referral fee (typically 25% of agent's commission).

**Getting Started:**
1. Connect with 3-5 local real estate agents
2. Ask about their referral program
3. When anyone mentions buying/selling, make the connection
4. Get paid when deal closes

**The Math:**
- Average home sale: $400,000
- Agent commission: 3% = $12,000
- Your referral fee (25%): $3,000

*Just 2 referrals per year = $6,000+ for sending texts.*

### 4. High-Ticket Coaching/Consulting ($100-500/hour)
**Best for:** Experts in any field

**Getting Started:**
1. Identify your expertise (career, fitness, business, relationships, etc.)
2. Define your ideal client (who needs your help most?)
3. Create a simple offer (1:1 coaching, group program)
4. Share your message on social media
5. Start with 3 free sessions → Convert to paid clients

**Pricing Tiers:**
- Beginner: $50-100/hour
- Experienced: $150-300/hour
- Expert/specialized: $500-1,000+/hour

**Building Your Pipeline:**
- LinkedIn content (daily posts about your expertise)
- Free workshops or webinars
- Referrals from past clients
- Podcast appearances
