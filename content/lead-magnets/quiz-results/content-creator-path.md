## Your Top 4 Opportunities

### 1. Freelance Writing ($25-100/hour)
**Best for:** Consistent income, variety of topics

**Getting Started:**
1. Create profiles on Upwork, Contently, and LinkedIn
2. Write 3 sample articles in niches you enjoy (finance, health, tech, etc.)
3. Start applying to jobs — 10 applications/day minimum
4. Accept lower rates for your first 3-5 clients (build reviews)
5. Raise rates by 25% after 10 completed jobs

**What Pays Well:**
- Blog posts: $50-200 each
- Email sequences: $100-500 per sequence
- White papers: $500-2,000 each
- Case studies: $300-1,000 each

### 2. Social Media Management ($500-2,000/month per client)
**Best for:** Creative people who understand platforms

**Getting Started:**
1. Pick 2 platforms to master (Instagram + LinkedIn, TikTok + Twitter, etc.)
2. Manage your own accounts impressively for 30 days
3. Offer free 1-week trial to 3 local businesses
4. Convert trials to paid clients ($500-1,500/month each)
5. 4 clients = $2,000-6,000/month

**What Clients Need:**
- Content calendar creation
- Post writing and scheduling
- Engagement and community management
- Basic analytics reporting

### 3. Copywriting ($50-200/hour)
**Best for:** Persuasive writers who understand selling

**Getting Started:**
1. Study copywriting basics (free resources: Copyblogger, AWAI)
2. Rewrite 5 existing ads/landing pages as portfolio samples
3. Target e-commerce brands and course creators
4. Charge per project, not hourly (you get faster, you earn more)

**What Pays Well:**
- Sales pages: $500-5,000
- Email campaigns: $200-1,000
- Ad copy: $100-500 per ad set
- Product descriptions: $25-100 each

### 4. Starting a Newsletter ($0-10,000+/month)
**Best for:** Building long-term passive income

**Getting Started:**
1. Pick a niche you can write about weekly
2. Start free with Substack or Beehiiv
3. Publish consistently for 3-6 months
4. Monetize via paid subscriptions, sponsorships, or affiliate links
5. 1,000 true fans × $5/month = $5,000/month
