## Your Top 4 Opportunities

### 1. Freelance Web Development ($50-150/hour)
**Best for:** Anyone who can build or maintain websites

**Getting Started:**
1. Build 3 portfolio sites (can be fictional businesses)
2. Create profiles on Upwork, Toptal, and LinkedIn
3. Start with WordPress maintenance jobs (easy, consistent)
4. Move to custom builds as you gain reviews
5. Specialize in a niche (e-commerce, SaaS, local business)

**What Pays Well:**
- WordPress site: $500-3,000
- E-commerce setup: $1,000-10,000
- Custom web apps: $5,000-50,000+
- Monthly maintenance: $100-500/month per client

### 2. Tech Virtual Assistant ($25-50/hour)
**Best for:** Organized people who understand tools

**Getting Started:**
1. List all tools you know (Google Workspace, Notion, Slack, Zapier, etc.)
2. Create a "Tech VA" service offering
3. Target entrepreneurs and small business owners
4. Offer to set up and manage their tech stack
5. Charge monthly retainer ($500-2,000/month)

**Services to Offer:**
- Email/calendar management
- CRM setup and maintenance
- Automation setup (Zapier, Make)
- Data organization and reporting
- Tool integrations

### 3. Online Tech Tutoring ($40-100/hour)
**Best for:** Patient explainers who like teaching

**Getting Started:**
1. Sign up on Wyzant, Varsity Tutors, and Preply
2. List your tech skills as subjects
3. Offer lessons in: coding basics, Excel, specific software
4. Target professionals who need to upskill
5. Build recurring students for consistent income

**Hot Topics:**
- Excel/Google Sheets (always in demand)
- Python basics
- Data analysis
- No-code tools (Notion, Airtable)
- Specific software training

### 4. No-Code App Building ($1,000-10,000/project)
**Best for:** Problem solvers who see inefficiencies

**Getting Started:**
1. Master one no-code platform (Bubble, Glide, or Softr)
2. Build 3 demo apps for your portfolio
3. Find businesses with manual processes
4. Propose automation solutions
5. Charge per project or monthly for maintenance

**Tools to Learn:**
- **Bubble** — Full web apps
- **Glide** — Mobile apps from spreadsheets
- **Airtable** — Database + automation
- **Zapier** — Connect everything
