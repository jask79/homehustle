## Your Top 4 Opportunities

### 1. Print-on-Demand ($500-5,000+/month)
**Best for:** Designers who want passive income

**How It Works:**
You create designs. They go on t-shirts, mugs, posters, phone cases. When someone buys, the company prints and ships. You collect profit.

**Getting Started:**
1. Create accounts on Printful, Redbubble, and Merch by Amazon
2. Design 20-50 simple designs (quotes, graphics, niche humor)
3. Upload to multiple platforms
4. Promote on social media or let organic search work
5. Scale what sells, drop what doesn't

**What Sells:**
- Niche humor (nurses, teachers, dog moms)
- Trending topics (memes, current events)
- Clean typography quotes
- Fan art (careful with copyright)

**Real Numbers:**
- 50 designs × $2 profit average × 100 sales/month = $200/month
- 200 designs × $3 profit × 500 sales/month = $1,500/month
- Top sellers: $5,000-20,000/month

### 2. Selling Digital Products ($100-10,000/month)
**Best for:** Creators who want to make once, sell forever

**What to Sell:**
- Templates (Canva, Notion, Figma)
- Presets (Lightroom, video editing)
- Digital art and illustrations
- Planners and worksheets
- Social media templates

**Where to Sell:**
- **Etsy** — Built-in traffic, 6.5% fees
- **Gumroad** — Simple setup, 10% fees
- **Creative Market** — Designer-focused
- **Your own site** — Keep 100% (Shopify, WooCommerce)

**Getting Started:**
1. Research what's selling on Etsy (look for 1,000+ reviews)
2. Create your own version (better, different, or more niche)
3. List with great mockups and SEO titles
4. Promote on Pinterest + Instagram
5. Create bundles to increase average order

### 3. Social Media Content Creation ($500-5,000/month)
**Best for:** People who understand what performs online

**Options:**
- **UGC Creator:** Brands pay you to create content they post ($100-500/video)
- **Influencer:** Build your own audience, get sponsorships
- **Content Agency:** Create content for multiple brands

**Getting Started as UGC Creator:**
1. Create 5-10 sample videos (product reviews, unboxings, tutorials)
2. Post portfolio on TikTok, Instagram, and a simple website
3. Reach out to brands (DM or email)
4. Use UGC platforms: Billo, Trend, JoinBrands
5. Charge $100-300 per video (raise with experience)

### 4. Etsy or Creative Marketplaces ($500-10,000+/month)
**Best for:** Makers of physical or digital goods

**Best-Selling Categories:**
- Digital downloads (instant delivery, no shipping)
- Personalized items (custom names, dates)
- Handmade jewelry and accessories
- Home decor
- Stickers and paper goods

**Getting Started:**
1. Research top sellers in your niche
2. Create 10-20 listings
3. Use all 13 tags per listing (SEO)
4. Get first 10 sales (ask friends, run sales)
5. Reviews drive more sales — deliver quality
