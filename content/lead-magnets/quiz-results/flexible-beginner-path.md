## Your Top 4 Opportunities

### 1. Freelancing on Fiverr/Upwork ($10-50/hour)
**Best for:** Learning while earning

**Beginner-Friendly Services:**
- Data entry ($10-20/hour)
- Virtual assistance ($12-25/hour)
- Transcription ($15-30/hour)
- Research tasks ($15-25/hour)
- Basic video editing ($15-40/hour)
- Social media posting ($10-20/hour)

**Getting Started:**
1. Create free accounts on Fiverr and Upwork
2. Pick 2-3 services you can do today
3. Create your profile (be honest about experience level)
4. Set low prices initially (build reviews fast)
5. Deliver great work → Get reviews → Raise prices

**Path to $500/week:**
- Week 1-2: Land first 3-5 clients at low rates
- Week 3-4: Get 5-star reviews, raise rates 25%
- Month 2: 10-15 hours/week at $20-25/hour = $200-375/week
- Month 3: 20 hours/week at $25-30/hour = $500-600/week

### 2. Virtual Assistant ($15-30/hour)
**Best for:** Organized people who learn fast

**What VAs Do:**
- Email management
- Calendar scheduling
- Travel booking
- Data entry
- Customer service
- Social media posting
- Research

**Getting Started:**
1. List every admin task you've ever done (work, personal, volunteer)
2. Create a VA profile on Belay, Time Etc, or Upwork
3. Take free courses (HubSpot, Google Workspace)
4. Start with one client, deliver exceptionally
5. Add clients until you hit your income goal

**The Math:**
- 5 hours/week × 4 clients × $20/hour = $400/week
- 10 hours/week × 3 clients × $25/hour = $750/week

### 3. Online Tutoring ($15-50/hour)
**Best for:** Patient people who know *something* well

**You Can Tutor:**
- Any subject you did well in school
- English conversation (for non-native speakers)
- Test prep (SAT, ACT, GRE)
- Music instruments
- Languages you speak
- Computer basics

**Getting Started:**
1. Sign up on Wyzant, Tutor.com, Preply, or Outschool
2. Pick subjects you're confident teaching
3. Set competitive rates ($15-25/hour to start)
4. Get your first 5 students (offer discounts)
5. Collect reviews, raise rates

**No Degree Required:**
- Wyzant: No degree needed
- Outschool: No degree for group classes
- Preply: No degree for language tutoring
- Cambly: Just be a native English speaker

### 4. Delivery & Gig Work ($15-25/hour)
**Best for:** Flexible schedule, immediate income

**Options:**
- DoorDash/Uber Eats (food delivery)
- Instacart/Shipt (grocery shopping)
- Amazon Flex (package delivery)
- TaskRabbit (local tasks and errands)
- Rover (pet sitting)

**Getting Started:**
1. Sign up for 2-3 apps today
2. Complete background checks (2-7 days)
3. Start accepting gigs
4. Learn which hours and areas pay best
5. Optimize for $/hour, not just staying busy

**Maximizing Earnings:**
- Multi-app during slow times
- Work peak hours (lunch, dinner, weekends)
- Cherry-pick profitable orders
- Track mileage for tax deductions
